Indie pop / indie rock with endless‑summer & celestial undertones.

About

Still Pond is an indie music project blending hazy guitars, melodic hooks, and reflective lyrics. The songs live somewhere between nostalgia and forward motion — chasing moments that feel suspended in time.

New music is released independently, shaped by late‑night demos, long drives, and the feeling that something good might be just over the horizon.
